How I work ?

My workflow is structured to ensure efficiency, collaboration, and continuous improvement, from planning and creation to analysis and optimization.

1. BRIEFING:

I often collaborate with agencies using Trello to organize and plan social media posts, aligning each post with editorial pillars and campaign objectives.

2. CREATION:

I create prototypes to visualize ideas, using Adobe XD for landing pages and Photoshop for visuals. If needed, I coordinate with agencies to ensure visuals align with our brand guidelines.

3. REPORTINGS:

I develop dashboards in Looker Studio to analyze performance, identify what worked and what didn’t, and adjust our digital strategy accordingly.

4. OPTIMIZATION:

Using data insights, I continuously optimize content and campaigns to improve engagement, performance, and ROI, ensuring strategies evolve with audience needs.
