Discover My Main Projects

I've had the opportunity to work on various impactful campaigns across multiple industries and markets,
from beauty to sports and agriculture. Here are a few highlights:

Yves Rocher: Famille Bio Campaign

I helped create awareness and engagement for Yves Rocher’s Famille Bio range through teasing posts, engaging quizzes, influencer partnerships, and product highlights. My focus was on crafting content that resonated with family-oriented consumers and promoting eco-responsible practices.

For Bridgestone, I worked on a digital strategy for the Olympic Games of Paris 2024, using social media, landing pages, and newsletters to retain participants and keep them informed throughout the event.

I supported Kverneland’s digital 360° campaign by increasing product visibility through targeted ads on YouTube and Google, and ensuring a seamless user experience via a dedicated landing page.
